About Vancouver Rec Explorer

Discover activities and programs across all community centres.

circle

Vancouver Rec Explorer was born out of a desire to bridge the gap between our city's vibrant community centers and the residents who call Vancouver home. Our mission is to simplify the discovery of local activities, making it easier than ever for neighbors to connect, learn, and stay active together.

From community yoga in Kitsilano to pottery workshops in Mount Pleasant, we bring the heart of our community to your screen. We believe that active living is the cornerstone of a healthy, connected city. By providing a refined and accessible platform for exploring recreational opportunities, we hope to foster a sense of belonging and encourage every Vancouverite to explore the wealth of resources available in their own backyard.

Join us as we build a more active and engaged community, one activity at a time.
